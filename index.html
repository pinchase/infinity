KNOW ABOUT US

Infinity Global Foundation seeks to provide an opportunity for improving the quality of life for the youth in Kenya and beyond through their empowerment and participation in economic and democratic processes as well as in community and civic affairs. It also advocates for creation of a supportive social, cultural, economic and political environment that will empower the youth to be active actors in community development

WHAT WE DO

Take a look at the programs and initiatives at IGFO

Free Youth Academy

Offering free online training in high-demand fields like Computer Programming, Web Design, Web Development, Graphic Design, Data Analysis, Content Writing, and Digital Marketing. This program equips youth aged 18-35 with essential digital and professional skills for career growth.

GAIN (Green Action Initiative)

Empowering youth to become environmental champions and contribute to sustainable development goals (SDGs) through hands-on projects, advocacy, and partnerships with public and private sectors.

Theatre Plus

Engaging youth through the Speaker's Bureau, Theatre for Development, and Weekend Challenges, combining education, performances, and motivational talks to address societal issues affecting students.

Talent development

Nurturing young talent in arts, sports, and entrepreneurship with workshops, talent competitions, awards, and festivals, while providing exposure and mentorship.

INIT (Innovation, Invention, and Technology)

Fostering creativity, innovation, and technological advancement among youth through hackathons, innovation labs, and mentorship programs

Podcast Program

A platform addressing youth issues such as mental health, education, leadership, and more, featuring discussions with guest speakers and real-life stories to inspire and inform young people.
